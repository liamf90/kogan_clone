Overview

Immerse yourself in the deep lows and crystal-clear highs of audiophile-grade sound with these wireless earbuds, filling your day with up to 24 hours of listening time with the Qi wireless charging case.

  • Fully immersive bass and crystal-clear highs thanks to the graphene diaphragm
  • 400mAh Qi Wireless charging case provides up to 24 hours playback time
  • Talk or listen up to 12 hours on a single charge
  • Built-in microphone for hands-free calls
  • Siri and Google Assistant compatibility
  • Superior sound quality for iPhones with Advanced Audio Coding (AAC)
  • Touch controls for volume, music and instant accepting of calls
  • Bluetooth 5.0 with BES2300 premium Bluetooth chipset
  • Charge wirelessly or via the Micro USB charging port
  • 2019 RedDot award winner
  • IPX6-Rated water resistance

Be swept away by superior sound, take calls in crystal-clear clarity and listen for longer with the Kogan T3 True Wireless Earbuds with Wireless Charging Case, boasting graphene diaphragm technology for a game-changing listening experience.

Graphene diaphragm

Unlike traditional mechanical earphones, these earphones use a graphene diaphragm to produce next-level frequency for intense sound without distortion, delivering more immersive bass and clear mid-high tones.

Qi Wireless charging

Charge your earphones fully up to two times for up to 24 hours of continuous wireless audio with the Qi-compatible wireless charging case, and easily top up the case and earphones simultaneously by placing your earphones inside and setting down on a Qi-compatible charging mat

Seamlessly call and connect

Simply use the touch controls to accept calls, or take and make calls in crystal-clear clarity on either earphone using the built-in microphone.
